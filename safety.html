Safety tips

Stay safe when meeting, messaging, and trading. These guidelines keep every swap respectful and secure.

Meet smart

  • Choose well-lit, public locations; bring a friend if possible.
  • Share meeting details with someone you trust.
  • Avoid exchanging sensitive info before you build trust.

Verify people and items

  • Check profiles and ratings; decline trades that feel off.
  • Inspect items in person before completing the swap.
  • Test electronics briefly; confirm sizes and condition.

Handle payments carefully

  • Only add cash adjustments you’re comfortable with.
  • Avoid wire transfers or sharing banking details.
  • Report payment concerns to safety@swap.local.

Respect boundaries

  • Keep communication on-topic and professional.
  • If someone asks to stop contacting them, respect it immediately.
  • Report harassment or suspicious behavior through support.

Need help?

If something feels unsafe, pause the swap and reach out at safety@swap.local. In emergencies, contact local authorities first.
