Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 4, 2026. How we collect, use, and safeguard your information while you trade locally on swap.

1. Data Collection & Purpose

We collect only the minimum data required to facilitate local swaps:

  • Authentication: Name/Email via Apple or Google OAuth.
  • Coarse Location: Neighborhood-level data (e.g., “Ballard,” “Capitol Hill”) to show nearby matches. We never store or share precise GPS coordinates.
  • Age Signals: We receive a signal from Apple confirming your age category to ensure compliance with 2026 youth safety laws.

2. Your Rights & Deletion (Guideline 5.1.1)

Account deletion and data purge:

  • Account Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data instantly via Settings > Privacy > Delete Account in the app.
  • Data Purge: On request, we purge personal identifiers, chat history, and listing images from production servers within 72 hours.

3. No Sale of Data

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use third-party tracking pixels or IDFA-based advertising.

4. Safety Logs

We retain encrypted logs of reported content to maintain community safety and comply with the 2026 App Store Accountability Acts.
