Privacy Notice

How we collect, use, and safeguard your information while you trade locally on swap.

Data we collect

  • Account details: name, email, and authentication data.
  • Usage signals: pages viewed, actions taken, and basic device information.
  • Location signals: optional, to surface nearby swaps when enabled by you.
  • Communications: messages or support requests you send to us.

How we use data

  • Operate the platform: matchmaking, messaging, and safety checks.
  • Improve reliability: diagnostics, fraud prevention, and abuse mitigation.
  • Communicate with you: account notices, safety updates, and support responses.

Retention

We keep data only as long as needed for the purposes above, unless longer retention is required by law or to resolve disputes. When data is no longer needed, we delete or de-identify it.

Security

We use encrypted transport, access controls, and monitoring to protect your data. No system is perfect; please use strong passwords and be mindful of the information you share.

Your choices

  • Access and update your profile information in-app when available.
  • Control location sharing via your device settings.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications via email preferences.
  • Request deletion by emailing privacy@swap.local.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at privacy@swap.local.
