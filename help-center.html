Help Center

Answers to common questions. If you need more help, reach us at support@swap.local.

Popular topics

Getting started

Create your profile, list an item, and browse nearby swaps. Keep descriptions clear and honest.

Finding a match

Use filters and map view. Message with intent and propose a time and place early.

Disputes

Start by messaging the other party respectfully. If unresolved, email support@swap.local.

Account access

Reset passwords via the login screen. If you lose access to your email, contact support for verification.
